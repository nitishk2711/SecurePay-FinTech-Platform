# Double Entry Ledger

Every transaction has:

- Debit
- Credit

Example

John purchases an iPhone worth $1000.

| Account | Entry | Amount |
|---------|-------|--------|
| John | Debit | $1000 |
| TechStore | Credit | $1000 |

Total Debit = Total Credit

Balances are calculated from ledger entries instead of directly updating balances.
